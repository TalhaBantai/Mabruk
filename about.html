About Mabruk Fire Safety and Security System

Mabruk Fire Safety and Security System is a leading provider of comprehensive fire protection and security solutions. With a firm commitment to safety, quality, and innovation, Mabruk specializes in the design, supply, installation, testing, commissioning, and maintenance of fire fighting and fire alarm systems for residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our Vision

To be a trusted name in fire safety and security solutions, ensuring the protection of lives and property through cutting-edge technology and expert services.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective fire protection systems while upholding the highest standards of safety and customer satisfaction.

Our Core Services

Fire Fighting Systems

Mabruk provides complete fire fighting system solutions that meet local and international safety codes and standards.

  • Fire Pump Systems: Supply, installation, and maintenance of electric, diesel, and jockey pumps.
  • Sprinkler Systems: Automatic fire sprinkler systems for commercial and residential buildings.
  • Fire Hose Reel Systems: Design and installation of hose reels and landing valves.
  • Dry/Wet Riser Systems: Installation of riser systems tailored to building requirements.
  • Foam Suppression Systems: Ideal for flammable liquid hazards.
  • FM-200 / Novec Systems: Clean agent systems for data centers and sensitive environments.
  • Portable & Wheeled Fire Extinguishers: Supply and servicing of all types (CO2, Dry Powder, Foam, etc.).
  • Hydrant Systems: Complete external and internal hydrant networks.

Fire Alarm Systems

Comprehensive solutions for early detection and emergency response.

  • Conventional Fire Alarm Systems
  • Addressable Fire Alarm Systems
  • Smoke and Heat Detectors
  • Manual Call Points
  • Fire Alarm Control Panels
  • Sounders and Strobes
  • Emergency and Exit Lighting
  • Voice Evacuation Systems

All fire alarm systems are designed and installed in compliance with NFPA and Civil Defense regulations.
