Our Products & Services

🔥 Fire Extinguishers

Used to extinguish various types of fire safely. Available in dry chemical, CO₂, and foam variants. Easy to use and essential in every property, our extinguishers come with safety certifications. We provide training to ensure you know how to operate them effectively during emergencies. Reliable performance with long-lasting durability for peace of mind.

🌀 Butterfly Valves

Controls water flow in fire protection systems. Built for high-pressure environments and easy operation. They are durable and highly resistant to corrosion, making them ideal for long-term installations. Tested to meet all regulatory standards for fire safety valves in both residential and commercial setups. Supports manual or automated control depending on your fire system configuration.

💧 Sprinkler Systems

Automatically activates and releases water when it detects heat. Installed in ceilings or overhead structures. Essential for early fire suppression, minimizing damage and saving lives. We offer both wet and dry pipe systems with tailored setup according to property type. Includes pressure regulators and smart activation options for energy efficiency.

🚨 Smoke Alarms

Detects smoke and alerts occupants with loud sound. Our alarms are sensitive and reliable. They feature backup batteries and wireless interconnectivity for consistent alerting. Perfect for homes, offices, and industrial buildings as part of a complete fire protection strategy. Installation is done by certified technicians following all local safety codes.

🛠️ Installation Services

We offer full setup and configuration of fire safety systems. Whether it's a small home or large factory. Installations include safety signage, fire alarm control panels, and escape routes. All systems are customized after inspection and follow building regulations strictly. Documentation and handover training is provided after every setup.

🔍 Maintenance & Inspection

Routine checks to ensure all fire equipment works properly and complies with regulations. We maintain logs, replace damaged parts, and recalibrate systems as necessary. Our inspection covers extinguishers, alarms, sprinklers, and suppression panels. On-call support and emergency services available for contract clients.
